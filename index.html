MISSION/PROBLEM STATEMENT/SOLUTION STATEMENT

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to collect some of the funds generated from Kenya's thriving tourism industry to create a comprehensive healthcare platform for tourists. This platform will ensure visitors access reliable healthcare services during their stay and direct a portion of the revenue towards improving healthcare infrastructure and services for local communities. The platform will have different feature like booking appoitment and enabling doctors to login and check for Tourist appoitments, patients they have , etc...

PROBLEM STATEMET

According to Ken Research, Kenya attracts 5,000 medical tourists. How can we optimize equipment supply chains and improve healthcare infrastructure to enhance tourist healthcare services, ensuring timely access to quality medical resources and bolstering the reputation of our region as a safe and well-equipped tourist destination, while also promoting efficient and sustainable healthcare system integration in 4 years.

PROBLEM SOLUTION

Our solution is to create paternship with local healthcare providers to establish dedicated tourist healthcare centers in key destinations. These centers will offer on-site medical consultations, emergency care, and referrals. We will also launch a hotline and a basic platfrom name "KTHG" (KENYA TOURIST HEALTH GUARD) for quick medical advice, teleconsulation services, etc...This platform will contain all healthcare information, receive appoitments from Tourists and enable doctors to login and manage systems such as; appoitment list, pending patients etc... Funding will come from a small healthcare fee included in tourist packages. Your support is crucial to implement these measures, improve the tourist experience, and benefit both visitors and local communities.
